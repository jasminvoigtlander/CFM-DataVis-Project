What is the dashboard?

The dashboard is a collection of your selected graphs from the graphs page. To move a graph about the dashboard for easier comparison for with another simply click the four arrowed icon and drag the graph to a new location on the screen.

How do I add new graphs to my dashboard?

Go to the "Graphs" page and click the "+" (plus) symbol to add a graph to your dashboard.

How do I upload new data?

Go to the "Upload Data" page a *fill out the form and click submit. Your new data point will be added to the data files that the graph reads from and included in the "Dashboard" graphs.
*be sure to select weather the included data is sale or attendance data.
**although not apparent, data will be uploaded to the file, upon successfull data entry.

What function do the filters play on the graphs page?

Filters allow you to only see graphs that match a certain criteria of data. If you only wanted to look at sale data, only the graphs pertaining to sale data would be visibile on the "Graphs" page.
